Work Flow

Finding the Reason

The first step in product designing or brand development is always knowing the reason behind the idea of the product or business. This enables us to create a robust product or brand that remains constant through out.

Brainstorming

The next step in the process is to determine the key features or the key adjectives that define the product or business.

Ideating
Product Design, Industrial Design

The next step in the process is to start generating rough ideas for product to include the key features identified in the steps prior to this.

Graphic Design, Brand Development and Logo Design

The next step in the process is to start generating rough ideas for brand name to include the key features identified in the steps prior to this.

Web Design

The next step in the process is to start greating wireframes for the content to be put on the website.

Version Development
Product Design, Industrial Design

The next step in the process is to start making different iterations for product, keeping user experience and usability at the peak.

Graphic Design, Brand Development and Logo Design

The next step in the process is to start making different iterations for brand name:

  • Typeface Selection
  • Color pallete
  • Logotype
  • Icon/Mark Development
  • Secondary Typeface Selection
  • Brand Application
Web Design

The next step in the process is to start creating website in following steps:

  • Wireframe generation to show the layout of the website
  • Designing the website for mobile screen access
  • Making the website responsive to include larger screens
  • Adding JavaScript, bootstraps, etc to make the page reactive
Demonstration and Testing

The next step in the process is to demonstrate the developed designs to the client for finalisation and testing for glitches and bugs.

Final Deliverable
The next step in the process is to deliver the final product to the client:
  • Product Design: Product Rendering and 3D Model.
  • Industrial Design: Product Design, Renderings, 3D CAD and 2D CAD.
  • Web Design: Hosting of the website to the URL selected by the client.
  • Graphic Design: Developed graphics or design in required format as requested by clients.
  • Brand Development: Brand Development Guide, PNG files, JPG Files, etc for the brand application.
  • Logo Design: PNG files, JPG Files, etc for the Logo.
