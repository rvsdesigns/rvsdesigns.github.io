About the Company

RVS Designs is a designing company making designs based on the principles of biomimcry, keeping nature at the helm.

The Story

In the summer of 2020, after being bored of lockdown, I decided to start providing the service of designing to others. As I have gained ample experience in product design and brand development in my short professional career of 4 years.

I have been making industrial designs both 3D and 2D for the past 4 years. But having the creative bone from childhood. I wanted to learn more about graphic designing and web designing.

Hence, I utilised my time during the lockdown to complete online course on Specialization in Graphic Design and Web Design for Everybody: Basics of Web Development & Coding."

Now, I believe that I am ready to make use of my learnings of Graphic Designing and Web designing as well as my experience of 3D and 2D CAD designing on Solidworks, Autodesk Inventor, Fusion 360 and AutoCAD.

This inspired me to start working freelancing in my free time to keep my creative bone satisfied and zen. Hence, The start of the company.

Lets Design to Inspire Change and Create Value!
